Who We Are

Zen Mind is dedicated to raising awareness about mental health and providing resources for individuals seeking support. We believe that mental well-being is a fundamental part of a fulfilling life, and we strive to create a community where everyone feels heard, valued, and supported.

Whether you’re looking for information on common mental health challenges, resources to help manage stress, or simply a safe space to explore your thoughts, Zen Mind is here for you. 

My Story

Zen Mind began as a small passion project in Lancaster, inspired by my own journey with mental health. After struggling with anxiety and depression for several years, I realised how challenging it could be to find trustworthy information and support. In the process of seeking help, I encountered many individuals who felt isolated, misunderstood, and unsure where to turn.


Motivated by these experiences, I decided to create a platform that would be more than just a source of information. I wanted to build a community where people could share their stories, connect with one another, and access resources that empower them to take charge of their mental well-being. From those early days, Zen Mind has grown into a supportive network of mental health advocates, professionals, and individuals united by the common goal of promoting mental wellness and breaking the stigma around mental health.


— John Smith, Founder of Zen Mind

Our Values

We approach every interaction with understanding and compassion, recognizing that everyone’s experience is unique. We believe in listening without judgment and providing support tailored to each person’s needs.

We are stronger together. By building a community where individuals can share their stories, connect with others, and access resources, we foster a sense of belonging and mutual support.

Mental health affects everyone, and our content reflects this diversity. We are committed to making Zen Mind accessible and inclusive, ensuring that people of all backgrounds feel welcome and represented.

Find Us

ZenMind’s primary location is in Lancaster, a city known for its rich history and strong sense of community. Our office serves as a hub for our team of mental health advocates and professionals, as well as a welcoming space for individuals seeking support. Whether you’re in the area or connecting with us online, we’re here to support you in any way we can.
